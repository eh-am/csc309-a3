How to use

Click on the buttons on the right to make the AJAX calls. The result will be displayed here.

Current REST API:

  • tweets/
  • users/
  • tweets?field=external_links
  • tweets/{id} Example: tweets/311828115477372928
  • users/{username} Example: users/timoreilly
  • users/{username}/tweets Example: users/SarahPrevette
